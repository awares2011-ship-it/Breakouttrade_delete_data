Account and Data Removal

Request deletion of your Breakout Trade data

You can request deletion of personal data associated with your Breakout Trade account or app usage. This page explains what to send and what will be deleted.

How to request data deletion

Send an email from your registered email address to our support team with the subject line Delete My Breakout Trade Data.

Email: breakouttradeapp@gmail.com

Please include

  • Your registered email address.
  • Your phone number, if you provided one in the app.
  • A short message confirming that you want your data deleted.

What data may be deleted

  • Name, email address, and phone number provided by you.
  • Account records stored in Firebase Authentication or Realtime Database.
  • Watchlist or saved app data associated with your user account, where applicable.
  • Notification token or app-specific user records stored by Breakout Trade.

What may be retained

Some records may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, payment dispute handling, tax/accounting obligations, or legitimate business records. Retained records will be limited to what is necessary and handled according to our Privacy Policy.

Processing time

We aim to process verified deletion requests within 7 to 30 days. We may contact you if additional verification is needed to protect your account.
